4. Analysis

Q.Compare the performance (time complexity) of Bubble Sort and Quick Sort?
Ans.
Bubble Sort=>
Best Case: O(n)
Average Case: O(n^2)
Worst Case: O(n^2)

Quick Sort=>
Best Case: O(n log n)
Average Case: O(n log n)
Worst Case: O(n^2) (but rare with good pivot selection)
